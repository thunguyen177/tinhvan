Truyn ng ngn x Baltimore

Ngy xa ngy xa, x Baltimore, c mt ngi tn l X nhn c mt bc th l t mt nh mi gii chng khon. Trong , ngi ta tit l rng c mt c phiu s tng mnh trong tun ti. Mt tun tri qua! ng nh li d on, c phiu y tng ngt ngng! Tun tip theo, X li nhn c mt bc th mi t nh mi gii ny. Tuy nhin, ln ny h d on l mt c phiu no s rt gi mnh trong tun ti. Qu thc ng vy! V c nh vy, lin tip 10 tun, v tun no li d on trong th cng tr thnh hin thc. Sang tun th 11, bn nhn c mt li mi u t vi nh m gii trn. i km vi l s tin mi gii khng l cho kh nng d on chnh xc nh c th hin trong 10 tun va ri qua th. Nghe cng kh hp dn nh?

Thc ra chuyn ny gn nh khng th xy ra. Thc ra, mi tun, nh mi gii n gi 1024 l th n nhng ngi khc nhau la o. Chiu tr l nh vy: Gi s xc sut d on chnh xc gi mt c phiu ln hoc xung l 50% th chuyn ny ging nh thy ng xu mi ln cho mi ngi anh ta gi th ti. Xc sut d on ng cho tun th nht l 1/2. Ln d on ca tun th 2 l c lp vi tun th nht nn xc sut d on ng cho tun th hai l 1/2. Ln d on ca tun th ba l c lp vi tun th nht v th hai nn xc sut d on ng cho tun th ba l 1/2... Nh vy, xc sut d on ng 10 ln lin tip l $$\frac{1}{2}\times \frac{1}{2}\times....\times\frac{1}{2}=\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{10}=\frac{1}{1024}$$

Nh vy, mi tun, anh ta ch cn gi 1024 l th i th trong chc chn s c 1 ngi nhn c th m c d on ng 10 tun lin tip!
