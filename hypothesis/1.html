Conclusion

It sounds strange but remember

You want to reject the null hypothesis and accept the alternative hypothesis.

So, we can say that we favor the alternative hypothesis more. This is completely opposite from the real world, where an "alternative" means something that is less favored, like "I love strawberries, but if strawberries get too expensive, then I can also get blueberries as an alternative."
