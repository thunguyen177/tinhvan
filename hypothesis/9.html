The average height of gnomes versus dwarves (testing for equal means)

 

 

There are two legendary creatures in a mythical land: gnomes and dwarves. There's a popular belief that gnomes are shorter than dwarves. But in recent years, gnomes usually add more vitamins and minerals to their diet. Despite of that, Dr. Fun still believe that the popular belief remains true. He decided to conduct a hypothesis testing procedure to see if the average height of gnomes is shorter than the average height of dwarves. So, he set up the hypotheses:
