Fixed: Error in get("state", pos_state) : object 'state' not found

When using predict with a linear model or using 'mice.impute.norm.predict', you may meet the error 
Error in get("state", pos_state) : object 'state' not found

This may be due to one of the linear coefficients is NA, which may indicates that the variable in question is linearly related to the other variables, not having enough observations to estimate the relevant coefficients(e.g. when $p>n$). If you predictors are categorical and you're adding interaction terms, an NA may indicate that there are no observation with that combination of levels of the factors.
