Online shopping for the dead world

I believe the spiritual world needs online shopping as well, and since my search engine startup for the ghosts may face many competitors, I need to have a backup plan. So, probably an online shopping store for the ghost instead? Lets call it the Dead Sea! But this has nothing to do with the salt lake bordered by Jordan and Israel, which you can walk on. I have no idea why they call Salt Lake a Sea, but I have a good reason for naming my shopping mall the Dead Sea: its a sea of products where the dead people can shop.

For new users at first, I don't know much about them individually other than their basic information. But since they opened an account on my site, they are probably interested in shopping anyway. So, after they hit the keyword in the search bar, I will return a list of ranked results based on the product's reviews and prices This is also based on a recommendation system, and the way it generates search results is fairly similar to how the returned results at my Noodle search engine are ranked.

Next, suppose that the user clicks on an item. Yet, I'm not sure if the product will satisfy my users. So, I will also generate similar products to the current item that the user is viewing to ensure that if he/she is not happy with the current item, he/she can quickly navigate to similar products.

 

But that's only shopping for one product. I need to maximize my profit. So, it is worth trying to get a ghost shop in a combo if possible. For example, I am to buy realization papers, which can turn hand-drawing into real things in a short amount of time. Then, I may be interested in buying a realization pen to draw on that type of paper as well.
